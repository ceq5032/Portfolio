Above are pictures taken from the game Samorost 1. Samorost is a puzzle point-and-click adventure game developed by Amanita Design. The game is beautiful and has relaxing music playing in the background. The small characters are interactive and upon clicking the mouse over them they make noises or perform functions. The puzzles in each level progressively get harder. I felt relaxed the whole time I was playing the game and trying to figure out my way to the next level.

The game starts off in outer space and this space rock is flying towards the main characters home. We never learn the name of the charter during the game. So, the main character sets out on his adventure to save his rock from catastrophic collision. His journey starts off in a spaceship which he lands on the asteroid heading towards his home and from there you are figuring out puzzles to advance in the levels to misdirect the asteroid.
